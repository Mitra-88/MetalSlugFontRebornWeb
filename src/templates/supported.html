Character support by font

Use this reference to see exactly which letters, numbers, symbols, and colors are available before you generate an image. It is especially useful when you are working with special punctuation or the uppercase-only Font 5.

Font 1

  • Letters: Lowercase and uppercase
  • Numbers: 0 to 9
  • Symbols: , * {} () ^ : $ = ! > - ∞ < # % . + & ? " ; / ~ _ | ¥ ⛶ © ♥ ▲ ▼ ◀ ▶ ⋆ ★ ☞ ✖
  • Colors: Blue, Orange, Gold

Font 2

  • Letters: Lowercase and uppercase
  • Numbers: 0 to 9
  • Symbols: , = ︷ ! - . + & ? / ♪ ✖
  • Colors: Blue, Orange, Gold

Font 3

  • Letters: Lowercase and uppercase
  • Numbers: 0 to 9
  • Symbols: ' {} () : , = ! > - < . + ? " ; / _ | ¥ ⛶ © ♥ ▲ ▼ ◀ ▶ ✖
  • Colors: Blue, Orange

Font 4

  • Letters: Lowercase and uppercase
  • Numbers: 0 to 9
  • Symbols: ' * {} () ^ : $ = ! > - < # % . + & ? " ; / ~ _ ¥ ⛶ © ♥ ▲ ▼ ◀ ▶ ✖
  • Colors: Blue, Orange, Yellow

Font 5

  • Letters: Uppercase only
  • Numbers: 1 to 9
  • Symbols: ! ?
  • Colors: Orange
Tip: if you choose Font 5 in the generator, your text will be converted to uppercase automatically.
